FAQ

Before you book.

Where are you located?

All outings take place at the 360 Bridge boat ramp on Lake Austin unless a prior special request has been made. Delivery to a home on the lake may be available with advanced notice on a case-by-case basis.

How long is a rental? Is there a minimum?

A two-hour minimum is typically required for Sea-Doo rentals. Boat rentals are two-hour minimums Monday-Thursday and four-hour minimums Friday-Sunday.

What is required to make a reservation?

The reserving person must be 25 years of age and have a valid ID and credit card. Depending on the request, a 50% deposit may be required. No special license is required.

Do you serve Lake Travis?

360 Splash mainly serves Lake Austin, but may fulfill a Lake Travis reservation by special request with advanced notice.

What is the cancellation policy?

To receive a 100% refund of your deposit, cancellation must be at least seven days before your reservation. Between seven days and 48 hours, 50% of your deposit may be lost. Within 48 hours, only store credit may be granted. Day-of cancellations and no-shows lose the entire deposit.

What if unfavorable weather is predicted?

Outings are not canceled due to forecasted rain or bad weather, only if it is actually raining at the lake at the time of the reserved outing.

Is fuel included?

Fuel is included in the cost on most reservations. Extended special requests are handled case-by-case.

Are water sports offered?

Water sports may be available at your own risk. Participants should be in good physical health before participating.
