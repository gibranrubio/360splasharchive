Lake Austin

One of Central Texas’ best days on the water.

Lake Austin is a Colorado River reservoir and a popular recreational destination for Austinites and visitors. The shoreline is almost entirely privately owned, which means you can see incredible lakefront homes from your boat or personal watercraft.

Launching near the Percy V. Pennybacker Jr. Bridge, better known as the 360 Bridge, gives you a close look at one of Austin’s most iconic pieces of architecture. You’ll also find restaurants, parks, fishing, and calm water for boating.

Parks

  • Mary Quinlan Park
  • Fritz Hughes Park
  • Emma Long Metropolitan Park

Restaurants

  • Abel’s on the Lake
  • Hula Hut
  • Ski Shores Waterfront Café
  • Mozart’s Coffee Roasters
